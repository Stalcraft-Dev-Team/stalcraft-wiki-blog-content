## Added
- tab "Active lots". In this tab you can find information about the availability of lots with the item at auction house: quantity, starting price, buyout price and time after which the lot will become inactive.

![active lots](https://stalcraftwiki-prod.b-cdn.net/releases/1.2.0/en/active-lots.jpg)

- background image for all pages except the home page.
- errors handling and displaying them on the site in a human-readable form.
